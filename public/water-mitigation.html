Water Mitigation

When responding to a water emergency we take pride in offering the best and latest technology in water extraction and water restoration services for the most efficient drying, dehumidification, and deodorization available today. Excess Water can cause personal property and structures to deteriorate if the problem is not treated immediately and properly.  In the event of an emergency, it is important to start mitigation within a 24-hour time frame to prevent further contamination and mold growth. Southern Restoration can be at your doorstep within 1 hour to start the process and get you on your way.  All employees are IICRC water certified.

CAUSES OF WATER DAMAGE

  • Broken water pipes 14
  • Roof leaks due to storm 2
  • A/C Overflow 1
  • Backed up septic pipe 1
  • No matter the cause we will find and resolve the reason 1

HOW DO WE FIX THE PROBLEM?

Analyze Damages

Immediately assess the damage and cause

Equipment

Bring tools and machinery that will properly dry the targeted areas

Sanitization

Sanitize the damaged areas to ensure mold or other microbials don’t survive

Removing Humidity and Moisture

Place commercial-grade dehumidifiers to remove any humidity or moisture in the specified locations (this will drastically decrease the chances of mold)
