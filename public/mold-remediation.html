MOLD REMEDIATION

If you see mold or think you may have a mold problem, it is important to call Southern Restoration Group. We are a licensed mold remediation company. The effects mold can have on the health of you and your loved ones is extremely dangerous. We offer quick, long term solutions that can free your home or office of mold in a safe and thorough manner. All employees are IICRC Microbial Remediation certified.

CAUSES OF MOLD AND HOW TO SPOT IT

Surface Condensation

Mold becomes present as a result of surface condensation due to excessive moisture, lack of ventilation, inadequate air circulation, water leaks, weathered wooden floors, and or flood marks
Places it can be found

It can be found almost anywhere and survives in moist environment

Its Appearance

The mold grows and becomes black, white, orange, green and purple organisms

HOW DO WE FIX THE PROBLEM?

Effective Barriers

Our process involves effective barriers to prevent cross-contamination using negative flow with HEPA scrubbers, mold removal, dehumidification and drying, HEPA vacuum, and disposal

Green Microbial

We only use a green microbial that is safe and 100% effective

Clearance Test

Upon completion of the job, we do a clearance test to assure the job was done effectively and efficiently
