Fire Restoration

The time immediately after a fire is extremely stressful, it is also extremely important. Fire Restoration should be performed immediately to prevent further damage from smoke and soot as well as water damage from the fire departments hoses. Southern Restoration has the experience to get your property restored to pre-fire conditions. We can get rid of the awful smell of lingering smoke and fumes, clean property of soot and ash, and use proper drying techniques to ensure mold does not grow from the water used to put out the fire.
